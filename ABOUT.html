Our Story

Founded in 2015, ProFix Mobile Repair began as a small kiosk in Lahore with a simple mission: to provide fast, reliable, and affordable mobile repair services with exceptional customer service.

What started as a one-person operation has now grown into one of Lahore's most trusted mobile repair centers, with a team of certified technicians and thousands of satisfied customers.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality mobile repair services with transparency, integrity, and exceptional customer service at prices everyone can afford.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's most trusted mobile repair service provider through technical excellence and customer satisfaction.
